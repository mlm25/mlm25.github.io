    Info

    The San Francisco Gem & Mineral Society is an educational non-profit formed in 1935. We are dedicated to organizing in order to create, encourage and develop the appreciation and interest of the earth sciences and lapidary arts.

    Affiliations


    The American Federation of Mineralogical Societies

    AFMS promotes popular interest in the Earth Sciences and sponsors and encourages the formation and international development of Societies and Regional Federations therein.





    The California Federation of Mineralogical Societies

    CFMS disseminates knowledge, establishes museum quality public exhibitions, and encourages the exploration, study and collecting in mineralogy, paleontology, lapidary, jewelry arts and earth sciences through fostering lesser associations in California.



    North Bay Field Trips


    North Bay Field Trips is an association of gem and mineral clubs in and around the San Francisco Bay Area, with the goal of sharing field trips. SFGMS takes part by hosting a trip to the club's "Spider Mine" rhodonite mine each year.
